African Food

Traditionally, wet beef fry is a popular african dish. Most people like it served with ugali which is also a popular african dish made of maize flour.

The recipe below shows how to prepare wet beef and with the right quantity of ingredients to be used for a tasty meal.

Ingredients

Serves 3 people

  • 3 big tomatoes, chopped into small cubes
  • 2 big onions
  • 1 kg beef
  • 2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp mushroom dark soysauce
  • 2 healthy pinches of Kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of beef masala
  • 5 stems of corriander
  • 1 cup of water

Preparation

  • Boil your 1 kg beef for 30 minutes until tender
  • Dry the beef then add 2 big chopped onions
  • Stir and cover to cook for 5 minutes
  • Add 3 big chopped tomatoes, stir and cover to cook for 8 minutes on low heat
  • Add 2 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • Add 3 cubes of royco and stir
  • Add 1 tablespoon of beef masala
  • Add a tablespoon of mushroom dark soysauce
  • Add a cup of water, stir and cover to cook for 6 minutes
  • Add corriander leaves and serve while hot
